Laccha Paratha

Ingrediens

  • Maida
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Knead a soft dough using whole wheat flour, water, and a pinch of salt; let it rest.
  2. Roll out, apply ghee, fold into layers, and roll again into a disc.
  3. Cook on a hot tawa, flipping and applying ghee, until golden and flaky.
